Oregon High School Tennis All-State Teams

Archive of All-State selections from 2022-2025

Note: Beginning in 2026, the OSAA will officially produce All-State Tennis Teams. This archive preserves the independent All-State selections made by OregonTennis.org from 2022-2025, the first and only media outlet to honor Oregon high school tennis players with All-State recognition.
Selection Criteria

All-State selections are based entirely on state tournament results:

  • First Team: State finalists (champion, runner-up) and third-place winner
  • Second Team: Fourth place, consolation winner, and quarterfinalists
  • Third Team: Consolation finalist, Round of 16, and seeded players who lost first match
  • Honorable Mention: Other notable tournament participants (6A and 4A-1A only, select years)
