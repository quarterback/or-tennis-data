2027 OSAA Tennis Format Modeler

Models the proposed 2027 individual tournament: a 16-team mixed doubles bracket replaces consolation in each classification. Mixed pairs are same-school only, and per the proposal only one partner must be a state qualifier — the other can be a rostered teammate who played the regular season and districts but didn't qualify (shown fictional, tagged +roster). Two streams fill the 16 lines — automatic district bids and provisional pairs that activate when the pair's state qualifier loses their first match (a two-qualifier pair needs both to lose). After singles/doubles round 1, eligible provisionals are sorted by averaged district finish (a non-qualifier counts as 5; lower finishers first) and backfill the open TBD slots; the rest are alternates. No team points exist in 2027 — this is its own championship. Everything is chalk-simulated and reproducible from the draw seed.
