How it works: Five independent computer ranking systems each produce their own team ordering. The Composite rank is each team's average rank across all systems. Spread measures disagreement — low spread means consensus, high spread means the team is controversial.

Elo — game-by-game rating updates, flight margin weighted. Colley — pure W/L, opponent-adjusted (no margins). Massey — least-squares on flight margins, capped at ±6. PageRank — authority in the win/loss graph. Win-Score — each win earns the opponent's win percentage.
