Fair Bracket Generator — OSAA-style individual tennis

Builds a state draw from district placements where byes are allocated strictly by merit (seeds → district champions → 2nd → 3rd → 4th), instead of partly by lot. Honors the OSAA separation rules (same district not in round 1; same school not before the semifinals; a school's district 1&2 not before the final). Works for any number of districts (4–8+) and any qualifiers-per-district. The right panel recomputes team points under the legacy round-reached scoring vs. a per-win scheme so you can see how much of the old point spread was an artifact of free byes.

Team points — chalk run (higher merit wins)

Round-based (legacy OSAA): 2 per main-draw win, a first-round bye worth +2 if the second-round match is won, +1 for the 3rd-place match. Per-win: 1 per main-draw win + a 4/3/2/1 podium bonus; a bye is worth 0. Both add the consolation bracket (capped at 2): +0.5 to reach its semifinal, +0.5 the final, +1 to win it.
